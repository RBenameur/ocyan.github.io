ABOUT OCYAN

"During the Coronavirus pandemic, educational continuity has been broken, and children have been left to their own devices. Literally.

This is particularly damaging for children whose brain development relies on academic stimulation, who no longer have classroom guidance, and are confined to their homes whilst lockdown takes place. Shifting curriculums online is not enough. It is tough to engage with nature whilst there are limitations.

The solution? Combine a remote learning resource with a sense of environmental responsibility that fills knowledge gaps on our Oceans in an entertaining and meaningful way. Through the ideas of four dedicated women looking to bring positive change and learning opportunities, Ocyan was created for those who need it most: our future leaders."
